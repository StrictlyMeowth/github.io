The AI Landscape

Who makes what, what it does, and how it all connects

Labs build the raw intelligence — models.  ·  Models are the trained brains: versioned, benchmarked, deployed via API.  ·  Apps are what you open.  ·  Agents act autonomously on your behalf.  ·  Creative tools generate images, video, voice.  ·  You interact with apps — not models.

Autonomous agents don't wait for instructions — they plan, act, use tools, and complete tasks on their own. The key distinction: a chatbot responds, an agent executes. This is the fastest-moving category in AI right now.
A parallel track running alongside language AI — generating images, video, voice, and music. Often overlooked in LLM discussions but equally transformative. Most major labs now ship both a language model and a creative model.
